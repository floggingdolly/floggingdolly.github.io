Description

Flogging Dolly is an action oriented endless runner with a novel control mechanism. The player controls a sheep by slapping it with a finger (in a similar way as slicing fruit in fruit slicing games). To maintain speed player must hit speed boosters. In order not to get eaten by a monster.

History

Flogging Dolly is Ivan Savu's pet project. Development began back in 2014. After four years of sporadic development, Flogging Dolly is finally ready.
