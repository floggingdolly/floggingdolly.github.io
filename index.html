An endless runner with novel control mechanism

Help sheep Dolly escape the monster, by slapping her with your finger (fruit ninja style) and guiding her over boosters.

  • Challenging gameplay

    Don't be fooled by its cute looks. This game requires some serious skill.

  • Fast paced

    The Monster is always close behind. No pressure.

  • Multiple backgrounds

    As you go higher, the scenery changes, reach the moon and beyond!
